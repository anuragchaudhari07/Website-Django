About us

E-Barristers is a legal knowledge and legal guidance platform where we try to resolve the grievance at a very initial stage before it becomes a conflict which at later stage is needed to be taken to a Court. We try to resolve the complaint at initial stage itself only. We provide pro-bono-legal and all other sort of legal services at economical cost. We provide injured consumers to exercise their rights , spreading awareness about their various rights and to guarantee them financial restitution originated in abusive practise of either telephone companies , consumer protection rights , various insurance laws, labour and right to information laws, legal notices etc.
